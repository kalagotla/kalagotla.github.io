Dilip Kalagotla

PhD in Aerospace Engineering

I am an aerospace engineer focused on integrating physics-aware deep learning techniques with computational fluid dynamics and experimental techniques to improve our understanding of fluid dynamics. My recent work incroporates data-driven techniques and numerical methods for shock-dominated PIV (Particle Image Velocimetry) data utilizing open-source tools (lptlib, syPIV, and BICSNet) achieving upto ~58% error reduction in mean flow velocity surrounding the shock wave demonstrating one of its kind framework to integrate bias-informed machine learning into experimental workflows.
