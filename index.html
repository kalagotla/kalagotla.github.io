Welcome to my personal website! I am a PhD candidate in Aerospace Engineering at the University of Cincinnati, specializing in computational fluid dynamics and physics-aware machine learning. My research focuses on developing innovative solutions for complex multiphysics problems in supersonic flows.

Research Focus: I develop physics-informed neural networks and advanced particle tracking algorithms to solve challenging problems in fluid dynamics, particularly in supersonic and hypersonic flow regimes.
